What is this app all about?

As the COVID19 pandemic goes on, we have bulit an inclusive platform that aims to create a safe space for trans and non-binary people and helps you connect with folks all around the world and build a community, on the occasion of Pride Month!
We have taken an initiative to spread awareness about the cause by adding a questionnaire that educates people and keeps the homophobes and bigots away from this platform

Moreover, they can inspire people or get inspired through the medium of publishing articles that share their powerful stories and read other's stories about their struggles.â€‹ 

Features

Educate

A dynamic questionnaire that every signing user has to mandatory complete that educates people and keeps the homophobes away

Connect 

Connect with people all around the world in our dynamic chatrooms which are very inclusive and act as safe safe-spaces

Articles

Read other people's stories and publish your own with our article functionality and get inspired as a community!
